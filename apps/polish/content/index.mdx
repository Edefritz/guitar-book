## Add to Home Screen and use even being offline

You can add Guitar Book to home screen and use app even if you dont have internet connection.

> **IOS:** To add Guitar Book on IOS, open safari with Guitar Book app, click middle bottom button, scroll down and click 'Add to Home Screen'.

## Editing songs

You can edit all songs by clicking button 'Edit on Github'.

## Use Guitar Tuner

You can launch guitar tuner by clicking button or guitar icon(mobile version) on top.

## Chords Preview

All chords which are on top of songs line could show you how to play specific chords.

> **Important Note:** All song which have chords in the same line with song text wont show you chords preview
